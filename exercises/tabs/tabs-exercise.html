Sports Activity Details

Team sports - Details

  • All team try outs will take place Mondays on the soccer field
  • Bring comfortable clothes and water to drink
  • All teams are co-ed

Aquatics - Details

  • Swimming lessons held every morning at 10 am
  • Swim team try outs will take place Mondays at 1 pm
  • All swimmers must shower before entering the pool

Archery - Details

  • Please ensure your parent or guardian has signed the special waiver
  • All archers must be 12 years old and up
  • Equipment rentals available at the front desk

Horseback Riding - Details

  • Riders must wear a helmet and boots
  • All riders must be 10 years old and up
  • Riders will be evaluated and place in groups based on their level
