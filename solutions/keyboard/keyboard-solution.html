About Our Chess Club

Camp Opeongo is a place where we celebrate exercising our bodies and our minds! We encourage all campers whether experienced or brand new to Chess to come out and try a game! We have knowledgeable staff to help you understand the rules of the game and a weekly tournament!

Tournament Information

On the last day of camp (Fridays) we will have a tournament for all players who sign up, grouped by level. If you are not sure of your level or are new to Chess we can help you figure out your level by the end of the week.
